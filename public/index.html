Tony and Linda Bonnici

The Tony and Linda Bonnici Scholarship Fund was established in 2010 to support students in Nevada. Through their foundation, they have awarded 293 scholarships, providing over $300,000 in tuition funding this year alone. Their commitment extends beyond financial assistance, offering mentoring, internships, and job placement support to help students succeed professionally.
